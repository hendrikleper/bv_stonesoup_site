Rules and requirements of CLUB 1

#1 Search for a home, a base. If you don’t find it, become restless and active.

#2 Be patient.

#3 Warning: Do not loose your personality. Remain loyal to yourself and other members.

#4 Become a Master in embroidery, knitting or other handcrafts.

#5 Listen to the anthems of the CLUB, every day, at least once.

  • FEVER RAY/KEEP THE STREETS EMPTY
  • THE KNIFE/HIGH SCHOOL POEM
  • M.I.A./M.I.A.

#6 Be independent, think for yourself.

#7 Salute the flag when arriving at the CLUBhouse.
